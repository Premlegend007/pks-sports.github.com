About PKS Sports

Learn about our mission and the team behind your favorite sports equipment

Our Story

PKS Sports was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide athletes of all levels with premium quality equipment that enhances performance and enjoyment of their favorite sports.

What began as a small shop with a passion for sports has grown into a trusted brand serving athletes across the country. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction remains at the heart of everything we do.

Over the years, we've expanded our product range to cover all major sports including football, basketball, tennis, swimming, and more. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has remained at the core of everything we do.

Today, PKS Sports serves thousands of customers worldwide, from amateur enthusiasts to professional athletes. We take pride in our carefully curated selection of products from the world's leading sports brands, as well as our own exclusive line of equipment.

Our Mission

To inspire and enable athletic achievement through quality equipment, expert advice, and exceptional customer service. We believe that the right gear can make all the difference in performance and enjoyment of any sport.

Our Values

  • Quality: We never compromise on the quality of our products.
  • Integrity: We operate with honesty and transparency in all we do.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek better ways to serve our customers.
  • Passion: We share our customers' enthusiasm for sports.
  • Community: We support local sports initiatives and give back.
